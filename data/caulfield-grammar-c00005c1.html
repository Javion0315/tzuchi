Caulfield Grammar

Caulfield Grammar School is a private Anglican school that offers year 1 to 12 education. The school has four campuses in the Melbourne suburbs of  Wheelers Hill, Malvern, Caulfield and Yarra Junction, and one offshore campus in Nanjing, China. Boarding school is also available for international students who wish to stay on-campus during their school years, with accommodation & other necessary facilities all provided.
